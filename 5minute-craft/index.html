====5 Minute Crafts====

Popular Art videos that are aimed towards children, are they really safe for kids? "NO" they are not!!!






====Crime/Allegation List====

Summary of Crimes/Allegation

  • Child Endangerment: showing dangerous "hacks" to children, for example, making white strawberries using bleach, or using a fork to fling hot sugar to make cotton candy
  • Misinformation: Some Hacks don't work as intended ,this can spread false information or dishearten children when they try their "hacks"
  • Dangerous Clickbait: Thumbnails such as the photo above can lead children to think things like hot glue on the tongue is safe
  • Giving false medical advice: this can potentially do more harm to someone if they saw a hack and follow it, thinking it will help their medical issue
