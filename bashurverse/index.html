====Bashurverse====

Brandon Ashur, known mainly as 'Bashurverse' was another of the popular cast associated with Skydoesminecraft. He gained over a million subscribers over the early 2010s. The main reason for his downfall was not because of current 'misconducts', but rather the exposure of an arrest he underwent 10 years prior in 2004. The backlash was brutal, and Ashur was driven clean off the platform as a result.






====Crime/Allegation List====

Summary of Crimes/Allegation

  • Allegations: sexual assault of a minor (related to the 2004 case but resurfaced in the backlash he received in 2015).
  • Alleged abuse: his ex-girlfriend Clara accused him of sexual and emotional abuse.
