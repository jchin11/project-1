====Jinbop====

Starlit Zhao, aka 'Jinbop' was a semi-popular minecraft youtuber who was mainly the "sidecast" to Skydoesminecraft in Skymedia. Seems to be a trend with association to Sky. Not long after he left Skymedia, Zhao would interact explicitly with a minor, ultimately resulting in his arrest by the FBI.

Summary of Crime/Allegations
